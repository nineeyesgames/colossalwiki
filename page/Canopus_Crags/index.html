Canopus Crags

The Canopus Crags (71°10′S 66°38′W﻿ / ﻿71.167°S 66.633°W﻿ / -71.167; -66.633Coordinates: 71°10′S 66°38′W﻿ / ﻿71.167°S 66.633°W﻿ / -71.167; -66.633) are a cluster of peaks of 3 nautical miles (6 km) extent, located between Vela Bluff and Carina Heights along the south side of Ryder Glacier, in Palmer Land. They were named by the UK Antarctic Place-Names Committee after the star Canopus in the constellation of Carina.[1]

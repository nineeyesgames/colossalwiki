Bonnie Clabber Bluff

Bonnie Clabber Bluff is a cliff located in Burke County, Georgia.[1] A variant spelling is "Bonny Clabber Bluff".[1]

Bonnie Clabber Bluff most likely was named after the foodstuff bonny clabber.[2]

Pyburn Bluff

Pyburn Bluff is a cliff on the Tennessee River in Hardin County, Tennessee.[1] The elevation of Pyburn Bluff is 476 feet (145 m).[2]

has the name of Jacob Pyburn, proprietor of a ferry at this spot.[3]

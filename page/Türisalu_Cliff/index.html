Türisalu Cliff

Türisalu Cliff is a cliff in Türisalu, Estonia. The cliff is part of the Baltic Klint and has a height of up to 30 metres (100 ft).[1]

Türisalu cliff has accumulated a negative reputation as a "popular" place for suicides, especially among younger people.[2]

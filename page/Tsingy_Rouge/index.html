Tsingy Rouge

The Tsingy Rouge (Red Tsingy) is a stone formation of red laterite formed by erosion[1] of the Irodo River in the region of Diana in northern Madagascar.

It is situated approximately 60 km south of Antsiranana near the town of Sadjoavato.

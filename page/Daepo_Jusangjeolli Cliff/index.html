Daepo Jusangjeolli Cliff

The Daepo Jusangjeolli Cliff (Korean: 주상절리대(대포동지삿개)) cliff is a spectacular volcanic rock formation at the southern coast of Jeju Island, South Korea. It is similar to the Giant's Causeway in Northern Ireland.

Jusangjeolli Cliff was created when the lava from Hallasan Mountain erupted into the sea of Jungmun. It was designated as Natural Monument No. 443 on January 6, 2005.[1]

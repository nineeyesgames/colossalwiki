Nalaikh

Nalaikh (Mongolian: Налайх) is one of nine Düüregs (districts) of the Mongolian capital of Ulaanbaatar.[1] It is subdivided into 8 Khoroos (subdistricts).

Unlike most other düüregs, Nalaikh is technically a separate city, but still under the common administration of the capital.[1]

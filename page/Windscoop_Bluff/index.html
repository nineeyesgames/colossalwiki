Windscoop Bluff

Windscoop Bluff (78°32′S164°28′E﻿ / ﻿78.533°S 164.467°E﻿ / -78.533; 164.467) is a rock bluff east-northeast of Birthday Bluffs on the south side of Mason Spur, Hillary Coast, Ross Dependency. The bluff rises to about 1000 m and is marked at the base by a large windscoop. The name was suggested by geologist Anne C. Wright, Department of Geoscience, New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology, Socorro, who examined the bluff in 1983–84.

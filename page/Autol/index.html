Autol

Autol is a village in the province and autonomous community of La Rioja, Spain. River Cidacos flows by the town. There are original rock formations close to Autol. The municipality covers an area of 85.28 square kilometres (32.93 sq mi) and as of 2011 had a population of 4458 people.[2]

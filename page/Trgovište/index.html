Trgovište

For other places with the same name, see Trgovište (disambiguation).

Trgovište (Serbian Cyrillic: Трговиште) is a town and municipality located in the Pčinja District of southern Serbia. According to 2011 census, the population of the town is 1,785, while population of the municipality is 5,091. It was founded in the year 1353 by Stefan Uros IV Dušan.

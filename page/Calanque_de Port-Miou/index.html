Calanque de Port-Miou

The Calanque de Port-Miou is one of the three big Cassiscalanques . It is very long and narrow, and thus was very suitable for establishing a marina.

The name Port-Miou is an approximate transcription in French orthography of the OccitanPòrt-Melhor (the "best port") which is locally pronuncied /pwɔʁ.mi.ju/.

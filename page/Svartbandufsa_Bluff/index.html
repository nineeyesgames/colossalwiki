Svartbandufsa Bluff

Svartbandufsa Bluff (73°29′S3°48′W﻿ / ﻿73.483°S 3.800°W﻿ / -73.483; -3.800) is a bluff at the southwest side of Tverregg Glacier in the Kirwan Escarpment, Queen Maud Land. Mapped by Norwegian cartographers from surveys and air photos by Norwegian-British-Swedish Antarctic Expedition (NBSAE) (1949–52) and additional air photos (1958–59), and named Svartbandufsa (the black band bluff).

Styggebrekkufsa Bluff

Styggebrekkufsa Bluff (71°55′S5°53′E﻿ / ﻿71.917°S 5.883°E﻿ / -71.917; 5.883) is a bluff overlooking the cast-central part of Austreskorve Glacier in the Muhlig-Hofmann Mountains of Queen Maud Land. Plotted from surveys and air photos by the Norwegian Antarctic Expedition (1956–60) and named Styggebrekkufsa (the dangerous-slope bluff).
